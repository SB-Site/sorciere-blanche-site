Discover White Witch Publishing: An Enchanted Bilingual Publishing House!

By The White Witch | October 1, 2025 | 5 min | Back to Blog

… An Associative House That Awakens Imaginaries

October 1, 2025 marks a turning point in the literary landscape with the launch of White Witch Publishing, a unique associative publishing house dedicated to creating bilingual French/English works. Driven by overflowing passion, our wish is to offer stories that transport beyond borders.

A Magical Editorial Line

We turn to the imaginary, magic, and spirituality through:

  • Literatures of the Imaginary: Unpublished or revisited tales, fantasy, fantastical, and dreamlike science-fiction fill our pages. Soon, comics and mangas will join this universe, exploring resilience and the quest for meaning in limitless worlds.
  • Bilingual Works: Each book, in French and English, celebrates the subtleties of languages. Translation, an art, preserves the beauty of the texts for your reading pleasure.
  • Esoteric Treasures: Explore ancient wisdoms, alchemy, symbols, spirituality, plants and their secrets, retold with a modern pen.
  • Our Blog: Share magical recipes, anecdotes, creative workshops, games, and unpublished surprises with us.

An Enchanting Launch on October 1, 2025

This memorable day unveils our first works, reflections of our love for diversity and literary quality.

If our vision inspires you, follow us on X, Instagram, and Pinterest, subscribe to our newsletter, and participate as our projects bloom online.

Together, let's bring the magic of words to life! Welcome to a universe where every scroll opens a door to the unknown, every story an enchantment.
