About Us

Dive into the enchanting universe of White Witch Publishing, an associative bilingual publishing house (French/English from 2026).

Dedicated to celebrating magic, creativity, and the brightest facets of humanity.

Our Enchanters

Virginia Besson Robilliard

Enchanted Quill & Founder

Modern Fantasy & Fantastical, Reimagined Tales & Legends, Humor

Biography

Virginia Besson Robilliard, enchanting quill of fantasy for over a decade, returns in a blaze after two years' absence.

Passionate author and visionary founder, she is the soul of White Witch Publishing, created to breathe life into tales that explore the beauty and mysteries of existence.

With a modern take on fantasy and esotericism, Virginia blends tradition and innovation to craft stories that transport and transform.

In 2025, she unveils the re-edition of *The Orphans of the Odyssey* for Christmas, followed in 2026 by the finale of the Armaciès sisters' adventures and the *The Irishman* series.

Expect a triumphant return of beloved characters and the tumultuous arrival of new faces!

Biography

Guardian of ancient knowledge and master of spiritual transformations, the Alchemist shares wisdom through writings that unveil the secrets of mystical herbology and alchemy.

His works guide truth-seekers on the path of self-knowledge and inner transmutation, blending ancestral tradition with a modern understanding of subtle energies.

In his nomadic workshop, scented with sage, rosemary, and lavender, this charismatic author crafts esoteric booklets.

His guides to potions and remedies, born from loving harvests with Dame Nature, are entrusted exclusively to our house.

Inspired by Celtic traditions and forgotten grimoires, the Alchemist invites us to reconnect with ancestral wisdom, far from New Age illusions, to awaken our inner divinity and find harmony with earth and cosmos.

Ravi D. Llibor

Technomage Storyteller

Techno-Fantasy, Mystical Science-Fiction, Hybrid Tales

Biography

Explorer of the boundaries between technology and magic, Ravi D. Llibor weaves tales where the future meets the ancestral.

Under a sky of stars and neon, this enigmatic author shapes science-fiction laced with mystery, where humanity encounters the unknown.

Technomage storyteller inspired by steampunk, cyberpunk, and cosmic visions, Ravi crafts hybrid universes where mystical science-fiction and techno-fantasy intertwine.

His writings invite us to rediscover magic in a world in constant evolution.

Our Editorial Line

Our publishing house offers a rich and varied palette of tales and tomes: fantasy, fantastical, romance, and humorous novels, mangas and comics infused with our signature magic.

We also publish booklets on alchemy, esotericism, spirituality, plants and their uses.

We re-edit forgotten treasures, carefully revised by our Alchemist.

Our publications include recipes, tips, games, leisure, and DIY projects to inspire creativity.

True to our ethics, we refuse any depressive, gory, horrific, or black magic works.

Discover our authentic and mysterious universe via our site, blog, and upcoming tomes, from October 1, 2025!
